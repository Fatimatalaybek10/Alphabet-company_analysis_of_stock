알파벳(Alphabet Inc.)에 대한 주요 정보: 티커: GOOGL (주주총회에서 의결권을 가진 A주) 본사 위치: 미국 캘리포니아 주 마운틴 뷰(Mountain View) 주요 사업: 구글은 세계 최대의 검색 엔진으로, 주로 구글 광고 플랫폼을 통해 수익을 창출합니다. 그 외에도 구글은 안드로이드 운영 체제, 유튜브(YouTube), 지메일(Gmail), 구글 맵(Google Maps), 구글 드라이브(Google Drive) 등 다양한 온라인 서비스를 제공합니다. 사업 다각화: 알파벳은 인공지능, 자율주행차(예: 웨이모(Waymo)), 클라우드 컴퓨팅, 헬스케어(칼리코(Calico), 베릴리(Verily)) 등 다양한 혁신적인 분야에 투자하고 있습니다. 
수익 모델: 주로 구글 검색엔진에서 발생하는 광고 수익이 대부분을 차지하지만, 알파벳은 새로운 기술 분야로 사업을 확장하고 있습니다. 알파벳은 여러 자회사를 통해 사업을 분리하고, 각 사업에 집중하는 방식으로 성장해왔습니다. 이를 통해 구글은 검색 분야에서의 리더십뿐만 아니라 다양한 기술 분야에서도 중요한 플레이어로 자리매김하고 있습니다.



Alphabet (Alphabet Inc.) is an American multinational technology company and the parent company of Google. It was founded in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin. In 2015, Google was reorganized under a new holding company called Alphabet.

Key Information about Alphabet Inc.
Ticker: GOOGL (Class A shares with voting rights)
Headquarters: Mountain View, California, USA
Main Business: Google operates the world’s largest search engine and generates most of its revenue through online advertising platforms. The company also provides services such as YouTube, Gmail, Google Maps, Google Drive, and the Android operating system.
Business Diversification: Alphabet invests in various innovative fields including artificial intelligence, autonomous vehicles (e.g., Waymo), cloud computing, and healthcare technologies (Calico and Verily).
Revenue Model: Although advertising revenue from Google Search remains the company’s primary income source, Alphabet continues expanding into emerging technology sectors.

Through its subsidiaries, Alphabet separates and manages different business areas independently, allowing the company to maintain leadership in internet search while also becoming a major player across multiple advanced technology industries.


# Alphabet (GOOGL) Stock Analysis

This repository contains two financial data analysis projects using Alphabet Inc. (GOOGL) stock data.

## Project 1: Return and Volatility Analysis

This analysis investigates key statistical properties of Alphabet stock returns, including:
- non-normality of log returns
- volatility clustering
- autocorrelation of log returns
- autocorrelation of squared log returns

The goal is to better understand the risk, return behavior, and volatility structure of GOOGL stock.

## Project 2: Deep Learning-Based Stock Price Prediction

This project compares several models for predicting Alphabet stock prices:
- Naive baseline model
- Multilayer Perceptron (MLP)
- Long Short-Term Memory network (LSTM)
- Convolutional Neural Network (CNN)

The goal is to evaluate and compare the predictive performance of different deep learning architectures for financial time series forecasting.
